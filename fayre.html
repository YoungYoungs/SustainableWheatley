Merry Bells Village Fayre

Saturday, 4th of July

The Merry Bells will be holding a Village Fayre in the Merry Bells building and car park on Saturday, 4th July 2016. The aim of the Fayre is to celebrate our community and to provide an opportunity for the whole village to come together and enjoy ourselves. There will be a hog roast, food and drink stalls, and local organisations will be invited to have stands representing and publicising their activities (in a similar way to the ‘What’s on in Wheatley’ events that were held seven or eight years ago).

Also, the event will require much planning and organising and the committe would be very interested to hear from anyone who would like to help.

For more details contact any of the Management Committee (see Merry Bells notice board for phone numbers/ emails) or email merrybells@btconnect.com
