Welcome to sustainable wheatley

Wheatley is a large village of nearly 4000 residents living in just under 1700 households. We are fortunate to have shops in our village centre high street and also a post office, library and a health centre. There are a number of preschools, a nursery school, and a primaryschool; a school for pupils with learning disabilities, a secondary school and the Wheatley Campus of Oxford Brookes University are all based on an exceptionally scenic site in nearby Holton. We also have a number of active churches and community groups covering a wide variety of interests. Various small businesses operate from the village, including a number of great pubs and cafes as well as a large hotel and supermarket on the outskirts. There are frequent buses running through the village going to Oxford, Thame, Aylesbury and High Wycombe.
